## Placing a Rune
Placing a Rune You can place a rune a nonmagical weapon, set of armor, or item. When you place an rune on an item, that item requires attunement. When a creature is attuned to it, the rune grants the wielder certain benefits as defined by the rune. An item becomes a magical item of the rarity of the rune placed on it when a rune is placed on it.

## Runes on Magical Items
Placing a rune on a magical item is exceedingly difficult. Magical items resist modification, and their magic interferes with the rune. It can be done with additional difficult as per the table below. If the item has attunement, the rune still requires separate attunement.

| Item Rarity | Rune Difficulty Modifier |
| ----------- | ------------------------ |
| Commmon     | +5                       |
| Uncommon    | +7                       |
| Rare        | +10                      |
| Very Rare   | +14                      |
| Legendary   | +20                      |

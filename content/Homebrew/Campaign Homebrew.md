## Gritty Realism

When out of an Urban Setting, a Short Rest can be taken once every 24 hours. A Short Rest is 8 hours. A Long Rest can only be gained by taking three consecutive short rests in the same location without interruption. Rest is interrupted by:

- Rolling Initiative
- Casting a Spell with a Spell slot
- Taking Damage

For either type of Rest, 6 of those hours must be spent sleeping and the rest can be light activity.

## Potions

Using an action to take a potion will heal you for the full amount of a potion (eg. 2d4 + 2 = 10). You can take a potion as a bonus action and roll for the healing. Feeding someone else a potion takes an action and they will still need to roll for healing.

## Fate

You can have a maximum of 5 Fate points. These points can be used to create allies directly beneficial to you in some way. You can spend a number of points according to the chart below. Please note you are responsible for providing the name, backstory, and purpose of the entity you are creating.

| Point Cost | Description                                                  |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| +1         | a single person                                              |
| +1         | a shop or business                                           |
| +2         | own one 1 specific item                                      |
| +1         | have a positive relationship with player                     |
| -1         | have a negative relationship with player                     |
| +3         | a faction                                                    |
| +3         | 1 free use of a class ability but gain 1 point of exhaustion |
## Fireside Chats

Fireside Chats are a roleplay tool primarily for earning I Know A Guy tokens. When two players are alone during downtime they can choose to chat. One player will pull from a deck with 1 of each suit. Depending on the suit pulled, they'll share a story from their character's backstory. After the Fireside Chat, the player who divulged a story will receive one I Know A Guy and all players involved will receive heroic inspiration

| Suit    | Story           |
| ------- | --------------- |
| Heart   | a story of love |
| Club    | a story of pain |
| Spades  | a story of loss |
| Diamond | a story of gain |

## Fame
Fame is the amount of followers you gain from continually posting on social media or performing on stage. It involves having a scaling fame die that dictates the size of your following. Every time you post, you roll your fame die and if the result is the highest number of the die, you Go Viral and immediately roll one die of the next higher tier.

Your fame consists of two features:
- **Follower Count** - number of fans that consistently interact with your content. This is a measurement of your overall fame
- **Fame Die** - The number of fans and interactions you attract through your posts. This is a measurement of your potential gains. 
### Going Viral: 
If you roll the highest number on your fame die, you Go Viral. Your fame die permanently improves and you can immediately roll your improved die to add to your current roll. 

### Fame Modifier
Your Fame modifier is the rate at which you gain followers. Your tier increases when you reach a new follower milestone or when your d20 Fame Die goes viral. 
Example tiers: 

| Tier | Follower Count | Fame Mod |
| ---- | -------------- | -------- |
| 1    | 0+             | 10       |
| 2    | 1000+          | 100      |
| 3    | 10,000+        | 1000     |

## Fame Actions
1. **Make A Post** - 
	- This is the most basic level of content creation. Roll your fame die once. Add the ( Result x Fame Modifier ) to your follower count
2. **Events** - 
	- Any performance or content can be an Event as long as you take the time to build hype over it
	- Sources of hype can be but are not limited to: Scheduling an event, Collaborating with another influencer, Advertising the event,  Having exclusive access, etc. You can have as many as three sources of hype.
	- When you perform an event, roll your Fame Die a number of times equal to the factors you've used to build hype. If any of those die Go Viral, you can roll the same number of dice again. 
3. **Sponsored Content** -
	- Occasionally, large organizations are going to want to work with you due to your influence. Sponsored Content will vary in payment but typically will scale based on your current tier of fame and the result of your Fame Die. Events can qualify for Sponsored Content as well.

Sending stones modified to connect to the ley line system for more efficient communication. Each ARCCom has a monthly fee to maintain and has the capability to communicate with other individuals and also connect to the Internet.

| Rarity   | Monthly Cost | Charges | Recharge rate (at dawn) |
| -------- | :----------: | :-----: | :---------------------: |
| Common   |     10g      |    4    |           1d4           |
| Uncommon |     25g      |    6    |           1d6           |
| Rare     |     75g      |   12    |          1d12           |


As an action, you can expend a charge to use one of these effects:
- **Send a Message**: You can send a message to another ARCCom user whom you have registered.  The message must be 25 words or less. The user hears the message through their device, recognizes you as the sender if it knows you, and can answer in a like manner immediately without spending a charge of their own. You can send the message across any distance and even to other planes of existence, but if the target is on a different plane than you, there is a 5 percent chance that the message doesn’t arrive. 
- **Ask the Internet**: You gain advantage on any Religion, Arcana, or History checks you are making for the next 10 minutes. Spending 12 charges instead allows you to research a topic and gain answers as if casting the spell Commune.

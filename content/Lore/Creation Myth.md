Long ago it is said that the planes of existence were separated by the Astral Sea. [[Deities]], known as the Founding Pantheon, held the cosmos in a system of balance until internal strife caused the Pantheon to lose control. An unprecedented disaster to the universe, the planes of existence began to crash into each other, causing destruction and instability in the material plane. Planes residing so close together lead to three specific changes in this world Post Merging:


- The merging of the planes resulted in a surge of arcane energy spreading throughout the world. Magic was much easier to learn for the surviving species and this lead to vast magical advances.

- The Founding Pantheon had lost their influence and power, their divinity fragmented and spread among many smaller gods and many of their religions dying in the process.

- Rifts leading to neighboring planes began to open all around the material plane. The most major portals have been contained using Planar Gates. Two Major cities in this continent have been established around the gates to The Nine Hells and The Feywild


While a lot of ancient technology was lost in the destruction following The Merging, civilization flourished with the new found arcane arts leading to leaps and bounds in technological advancement. The world is currently in a post Arcane Revolution era with ever evolving tech and industrialization.

After the Merging, divinity dispersed far and wide throughout all the planes of existence, finding purchase in anything it could. Deities in this realm are as varied as they are countless, with each one taking on their own domains and surviving off of any faith generated by their followers. A Deity's strength is typically determined by the amount of influence and followers they hold. However it isn't uncommon for the average person to worship many smaller deities of very specific domains. As a result, many dieties seek to increase their influence through a variety of means such as sponsoring heroes, running businesses that provide services, or performing minor miracles for free. Dieties who do not manage to gain enough favor can die out, eventually replaced by a new spirit who takes up their domain. Dieties who have reach enough influence become known as gods. There are currently 5 major organized religions in the country, each of which belonging to their own god. The current major gods are [[The Mourner]], [[The Mother]], [[The Giver]], [[The Smith]], and [[The Messenger]]

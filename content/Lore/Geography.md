There are two major continents in this world, [[Osnia]] and [[Aspal]], with a few major islands interspersed. Osnia is the continent where you guys will be beginning and it is split into three major countries.

Two of the countries are created around the major planar rifts with their capital cities located right at the gate. These countries are

- [[Sylvania]], connected to the Feywild, capital: Wylderrel (will- duh- rell)

- [[Iftrine]], connected to the nine hells, capital: Nillo (ne-yo)

  

The last country, which you all reside in, is the [[Stoneguard]] whose capital lies between the [[Shaderoot Forrest]], a dangerous and expansive woodland filled with magical creatures, and the [[Bulwark mountains]], a craggy and desolate mountain range. While previously the capital was isolated from the world due to its hostile surroundings, modern technology has allowed the capital, Egrax, to flourish and become a central hub of commerce and industry.

The countries have an alliance between each other to keep trade alive and well within the continent of Osnia and construction on an international highspeed railway has been underway.

# Introduction

Modern Magic follows the world of Agna where a long time ago, cosmic forces drove all the planes of existence to collide and jumpstarted an industrial revolution. This is a world where magic is more easily accessible and technology has advanced greatly. As we play more and learn more about the world, notes and details will become available to everyone here. 

We will be playing in DND 2024 edition. For the updated rules please head over to [This Wiki](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/)

## Lines and Veils
These are our hard and soft boundaries for roleplay in this game. Please keep them in mind as we go and let me know if you feel the list is incomplete.
#### Lines - hard boundaries - avoid at all costs
- Cannibalism
- Torture
- Gore and Body Horror
- Sexual Assault (including any allusions to)

#### Veils - soft boundaries - take care with these
- Spiders/Arachnids
- Self Harm
- Pregnancy / Dangers to Pregnancy
- Homophobia
- Racism
- Xenophobia
- IRL Religions
